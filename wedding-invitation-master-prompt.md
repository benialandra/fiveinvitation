# ANIMATION SYSTEM

The website must not feel static.

Every section must have unique animation.

---

## Mouse Tracking

Hero elements follow mouse.

Include:

* Layer depth
* Light movement
* Decorative movement

---

## Parallax System

Multiple depth layers.

Examples:

* Background
* Flowers
* Photos
* Decorative elements

All move at different speeds.

---

## Floating Objects

Use realistic floating motion.

Examples:

* Flowers
* Leaves
* Light particles
* Sparkles
* Butterflies

Movement must feel natural.

---

## Scroll Animations

Each section:

* Different transition
* Different entrance

Avoid repeating fade-in.

Possible:

* Slide
* Rotate
* Reveal
* Scale
* Mask transitions

---

## Photo Interactions

Photos must react to:

* Hover
* Touch
* Mouse movement

Effects:

* 3D tilt
* Reflection
* Dynamic shadow
* Zoom

---

## Smooth Scrolling

Use:

* Lenis

Goal:

Apple-level scrolling experience.

---

## Cursor Effects

Custom cursor.

Features:

* Hover state
* Interactive feedback
* Elegant transitions

---

## Dynamic Lighting

Background should have:

* Moving light source
* Soft gradients
* Ambient glow
